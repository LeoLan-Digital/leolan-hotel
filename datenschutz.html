2. General information on data processing

We process personal data in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and applicable German data protection law. Personal data is processed only to the extent necessary to provide a functional website, deliver our services, respond to enquiries, and analyze the use of our website where lawful consent or another legal basis exists.

3. Server log files and hosting

This website may automatically collect technical information when you access it, such as your IP address, browser type, device information, date and time of access, and requested pages. This processing is necessary to ensure the stability, security, and delivery of the website.

4. Contact enquiries

If you contact us by e-mail or through a contact form, we process the information you provide (such as name, e-mail address, telephone number, and message content) solely for the purpose of handling your enquiry and any follow-up communication. The legal basis is Art. 6 (1) lit. b GDPR if your request relates to a contract or pre-contractual measures, otherwise Art. 6 (1) lit. f GDPR based on our legitimate interest in efficient communication.

5. Cookies and consent management

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies where required for technical operation, user preferences, and analytics/marketing integrations. Non-essential cookies are only set with your consent in accordance with Art. 6 (1) lit. a GDPR and Section 25 TTDSG. You can withdraw your consent at any time with future effect via your browser settings or any consent management tool used on the website.

6. Google Analytics (GA4)

Provided that you have given your consent, this website uses Google Analytics 4, a web analytics service provided by Google. Google Analytics helps us understand how visitors use our website, for example by measuring page views, session duration, and interactions. Google may process data such as IP-derived location information, browser and device data, and usage events. IP anonymization and privacy-friendly settings should be enabled where available. The legal basis is your consent pursuant to Art. 6 (1) lit. a GDPR.

Provided that you have given your consent, this website may use the Meta Pixel provided by Meta Platforms to measure the effectiveness of advertisements and improve our marketing activities. This may involve collecting information about page visits, user behavior, and conversions. The legal basis is your consent pursuant to Art. 6 (1) lit. a GDPR.

8. Recipients and international transfers

We may share personal data with technical service providers and platform operators only where this is necessary for website operation, analytics, marketing, or communication. If data is transferred to countries outside the European Union or European Economic Area, such transfers are carried out only on the basis of appropriate safeguards, such as an adequacy decision or standard contractual clauses, where required.

9. Retention period

We store personal data only for as long as necessary for the respective purpose or as required by statutory retention periods. Data that is no longer required will be deleted unless legal obligations require continued storage.

10. Your rights under the GDPR

You have the right to request information about your stored personal data, rectification of inaccurate data, erasure, restriction of processing, data portability, and to object to processing where the legal requirements are met. Where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw that consent at any time with future effect. To exercise your rights, please contact info@leolan.net.

11. Right to lodge a complaint

You have the right to lodge a complaint with a competent data protection supervisory authority if you believe that the processing of your personal data violates applicable data protection law.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect legal, technical, or operational changes. The version published on this website shall apply.
