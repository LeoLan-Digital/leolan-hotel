The Founder

From idea to solution — based on personal experience.

On vacation, the children are asleep in the room. Ordering room service — impossible. You cannot make a call, cannot go out, have to stay quiet. And then there is a language barrier on top of that.

You are not willing to spend half an hour arguing just to explain that you do not want a regular blanket, but a light one. On vacation, you do not want stress. You simply want to show what you need — or write it in your own language.

That exact moment is what led me to found LeoLan Digital. I know the problems of restaurateurs and hoteliers — not from a presentation, but from real experience as a guest and as an entrepreneur.

Our approach: No complicated systems, no months-long IT project. We set everything up — you benefit from day one.
